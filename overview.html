About TLC Insurance

Locally owned and committed to protecting our community in Bucks County, PA.

Located in Bucks County, PA, we’ve been quietly protecting our local community for years. As a locally owned insurance brokerage, our focus has always been on people first, specifically helping families and businesses find the right coverage and peace of mind.

We specialize in personal, commercial, and life insurance, offering tailored solutions that fit your unique needs. Whether it’s your home, auto, business, or life, we take the time to understand your risks and provide guidance you can trust.

While our brokerage continues to grow, our commitment to exceptional customer service remains unchanged. We pride ourselves on being accessible, responsive, and attentive—delivering the kind of service larger insurers often can’t.

We’re here to help you protect what matters most, today and in the future.
