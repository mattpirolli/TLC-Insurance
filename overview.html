About Us

Locally owned and committed to serving & protecting our community

Located in Bucks County, PA, we’ve been quietly protecting our local community in Bucks, Montgomery and Philadelphia for years. As a locally owned insurance brokerage, our focus is on people first, specifically helping families and businesses find the right coverage and peace of mind.

We specialize in personal insurance and also support our customer's commercial and life insurance needs, offering tailored solutions that fit your unique needs. Whether it’s your home, auto, business, or life, we take the time to understand your risks and provide guidance you can trust. And of course don't forget about the toys! We provide coverage for boats, RVs, jewelry and most other assets you want to protect.

While our brokerage continues to grow, our commitment to exceptional customer service remains unchanged. We pride ourselves on being accessible, responsive, and attentive to our existing customers. TLC offers the direct and personal service large insurers often struggle to including annual reviews, direct access for policy updates and easy access for questions, updates or new quotes.

We’re here to help you protect what matters most, today and in the future.

We believe insurance is not one size fits all. To ensure we fully understand your needs and match you with the right coverage from our network of insurers, quotes are not available through our website.
